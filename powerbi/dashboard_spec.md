# Power BI Dashboard Specification

## Pages
1. Executive Decision: final INVEST / WAIT / AVOID by scenario.
2. Country Comparison: Russia, China, India, Kazakhstan, Turkmenistan.
3. Russia Deep Dive: macro, industry, sanctions, currency volatility.
4. Air Liquide Simulation: CAPEX, OPEX, ROI, break-even by sanctions scenario.
5. Data Quality: source registry and missing data flags.

## Core Visuals
- KPI cards: selected country EIRM score, sanction index, adjusted ROI, break-even years.
- Matrix: countries x scenarios with recommendation.
- Line charts: GDP, inflation, FDI, trade balance by year.
- Bar chart: industry/manufacturing share and logistics score.
- Waterfall: gross ROI to risk-adjusted ROI.
