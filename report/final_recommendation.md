# Final Recommendation

## Decision

**Russia: WAIT under partial easing; AVOID under current sanctions for a new Air Liquide-style industrial unit.**

## Rationale

The industrial logic is real, but current legal, payment and insurance constraints can destroy risk-adjusted ROI even when gross plant economics appear positive.

## Trigger To Move From WAIT To INVEST

- sanctions relief explicitly covering industrial gas equipment/service activity,
- bankable payment route,
- insurable cargo and operating risk,
- confirmed local energy tariff,
- customer offtake contracts with enforceable hard-currency economics,
- board-approved compliance framework.

## Portfolio Action

Prioritize India and Kazakhstan for active pipeline development while maintaining Russia as a monitored strategic option.
