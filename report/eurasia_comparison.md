# Eurasia Comparison

## Russia
Highest strategic relevance in energy-heavy industry, but the highest sanctions/payment/insurance risk.

## India
Strong growth, lower geopolitical entry friction and major industrial gas demand across refining, electronics, healthcare and manufacturing.

## Kazakhstan
Attractive Central Asian bridge with metallurgy, oil/gas and logistics relevance. Main issue is secondary-sanctions exposure and corridor dependency.

## Turkmenistan
Energy-rich but opaque. Data transparency, access and governance risks reduce near-term attractiveness.

## Western China
Industrial depth and infrastructure are strong, but foreign industrial investment must account for export controls, geopolitics and local competition.
