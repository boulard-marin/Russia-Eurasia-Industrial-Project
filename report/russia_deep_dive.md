# Russia Deep Dive

Russia offers strong industrial and energy fundamentals: upstream energy capacity, metallurgy, chemicals, rail depth and large domestic industrial demand.

Current barriers dominate the investment case:
- sanctions and export-control restrictions,
- international payment constraints,
- insurance limitations,
- legal uncertainty for Western-origin equipment and services,
- reputational and compliance governance requirements.

## Data Position

Public macro data is provided through World Bank indicators. Currency data is sourced from the Central Bank of Russia daily USD/RUB XML feed. Detailed plant-level energy tariffs, Air Liquide Russia customer contracts and insurable project-risk premiums are not publicly available.

## Decision View

Russia is strategically material but not currently a clean greenfield entry case for a Western industrial gas group. The rational posture is option-preserving monitoring rather than immediate deployment of capital.
